Missed Job Resolution

User stories for handling jobs that were never started — including pre-start reminders, same-day late signals, overdue timer escalation, and the post-hoc resolution flow for both the provider on mobile and the admin in the dashboard.

Provider Mobile app What the assigned provider experiences before, during, and after a missed job

Before the job starts

P–1

Get a reminder 10 minutes before the job starts

As a provider, I want an alert when my job is about to start so I can confirm I'm heading out — or let the office know right away if I can't make it.

Shipped T–10 in-app alert SMS notification

Acceptance criteria

  • When the job detail screen is open and the job is still assigned with ≤10 min until scheduledAt, a native Alert fires once: "Your job starts in X minutes. Are you heading out?"
  • Alert has two buttons: I'm on my way (default) and Can't make it (destructive)
  • Tapping I'm on my way triggers the On My Way status transition — same as the action bar button
  • Tapping Can't make it opens the no-show reason sheet directly (P–4)
  • Alert fires at most once per screen mount — no repeat on re-opens after the window has passed
  • Backend sends an SMS via booking.pre_start notify event in the same T±10 min window (scheduler sweep every 5 min, deduped by preStartAlertSentAt)
P–2

See that I'm running late on the same day

As a provider, I want the job detail to surface a clear warning when the scheduled start time has already passed and I'm still not on the way, so I know I need to act immediately.

Shipped Late banner Same-day only

Acceptance criteria

  • When status === "assigned" and Date.now() > scheduledAt (same calendar day), an amber banner appears at the top of the job detail
  • Banner headline shows live minutes late: "You're 12m late" — or "1h 5m late" when past 60 min
  • Banner body reads: "Not heading out? Let the office know."
  • Banner has a Can't make it button that opens the no-show reason sheet (P–4)
  • Banner is mutually exclusive with the Missed banner — the Missed banner only appears the next calendar day
  • Banner disappears automatically once the provider taps On My Way and the status transitions

While working

P–3

Clock out from the overdue alert

As a provider who is still working past the scheduled end time, I want to clock out directly from the "Still working?" alert so I don't have to dismiss it and navigate back to the action bar.

Shipped Overdue timer 30-min cadence

Acceptance criteria

  • "Still working?" alert fires every 30 min past the scheduled end time while the job is in_progress
  • Alert has two buttons: Still going (default) and Clock out (destructive)
  • Tapping Clock out triggers the same flow as the action bar button — including the checklist-incomplete guard and the early-clockout reason picker if applicable
  • Tapping Still going dismisses the alert and resets the 1-hour escalation window (see P–5)
P–4

Office is notified when I stop responding to the overdue alert

As a provider, I want the app to automatically alert the office if I haven't responded to the overdue prompt for an hour, so someone can check in without me having to reach out manually.

Shipped Escalation 1-hour window

Acceptance criteria

  • If 1 hour passes in extra-time without the provider tapping Still going, the timer stops automatically
  • The extra-time amber card is replaced by a red banner: "Timer stopped — office notified" / "No response received. The office has been notified to follow up."
  • No further "Still working?" alerts fire after escalation
  • App sends PATCH /bookings/:id/flag-clockout — stamps clockoutFlaggedAt and notifies admin (see A–2)
  • The endpoint is idempotent — a second call (e.g. on reconnect) returns 200 with no extra side effects
  • Tapping Still going at any point resets the 1-hour window from that tap

After the job — resolution flow

P–5

See that a past job needs attention

As a provider, I want a clear flag on any job whose date has passed without me clocking in, so I know I need to resolve it rather than just ignoring it.

Shipped Missed badge Amber banner

Acceptance criteria

  • Job detail header shows a Missed status pill when the job is past-day and unresolved
  • Amber banner reads "This job was never started" with a Resolve button
  • Banner only appears for past-calendar-day jobs in committed statuses (assigned, confirmed, in_progress, on_the_way) that are not completed or cancelled
  • Completed and cancelled jobs never show the banner
P–6

Report that I actually worked the job

As a provider, I want to log my actual start and end times when I worked a job but forgot to clock in or out, so the office can verify my hours and I still get paid.

Shipped Resolve sheet Time steppers Note field

Acceptance criteria

  • Tapping Resolve opens a sheet with two paths: "I worked this job" and "I didn't make it"
  • Selecting "I worked this job" pushes a Log actual hours screen
  • Screen shows ± steppers for clock-in and clock-out times; hours cycle 1–12, minutes snap to :00/:15/:30/:45, AM/PM toggles independently
  • Times default to the booking's scheduled start and end
  • A required Note field prompts a brief description for the office to verify against
  • Submitting with end ≤ start shows "Invalid times — end time must be after start time"
  • Submitting with an empty note shows "Note required"
  • Valid submission sends PATCH /bookings/:id/resolve-missed with resolution: "worked", start/end ISO timestamps, and the note
  • Success alert: "Submitted — The office will review and update the job." then navigates back
P–7

Report that I couldn't make it

As a provider, I want to quickly tell the office why I didn't show up — whether from a same-day banner, the pre-start alert, or the next-day missed banner — so they can handle the customer without me making a phone call.

Shipped No-show reasons Reason required

Acceptance criteria

  • The no-show sheet is reachable from three entry points: Resolve → I didn't make it (past-day missed), Can't make it on the late banner (same-day), and Can't make it on the T–10 pre-start alert
  • Reasons: Sick or emergency · Vehicle / transport issue · Wrong location or locked out · Scheduling conflict · Other
  • Submit is disabled until a reason is selected
  • Tapping Submit sends PATCH /bookings/:id/resolve-missed with resolution: "no_show" and the selected reason
  • Success alert: "Reported — The office has been notified. They'll follow up if needed."
  • If the job was already resolved, server returns 409 and app shows an error — no crash
Admin Dashboard What the office sees and does after provider signals or resolutions come in
⚠

The backend stores all resolution and escalation data and fires in-app notifications today. The dedicated admin review UI (stories A–3 through A–5) is not yet built — these stories define the intended experience for the next sprint.

A–1

Get notified when a provider resolves a missed job

As an admin, I want an in-app notification the moment a provider submits a resolution so nothing falls through the cracks after the fact.

Shipped booking.missed_resolution

Acceptance criteria

  • On successful PATCH /bookings/:id/resolve-missed, backend fires a booking.missed_resolution admin notification
  • Notification message names the provider and booking reference
  • Notification links to the booking detail page
  • Notification appears in the admin notifications panel without a page reload
A–2

Get notified when a provider's timer auto-escalates

As an admin, I want to know when the app has automatically stopped a provider's overdue timer because they weren't responding, so I can call or message them to find out what's happening.

Shipped booking.clockout_flag Escalation

Acceptance criteria

  • When the provider's timer escalates, app calls PATCH /bookings/:id/flag-clockout
  • Backend stamps clockoutFlaggedAt on the booking and fires a booking.clockout_flag admin notification
  • Notification message: "Provider did not respond to the overdue alert — booking #XXXX timer stopped automatically."
  • Notification links to the booking detail
  • Second call is idempotent — no duplicate notification if the provider reconnects
A–3

Review a "worked" claim and approve or reject it

As an admin, I want to see the provider's submitted hours and note on the booking record so I can decide whether to mark the job complete and count it toward their pay.

Pending UI Next sprint

Acceptance criteria

  • Booking detail shows a "Missed — resolved by provider" section when missedResolution is present
  • Section displays: resolution type, submitted date/time, provider's claimed start and end times, and the note
  • Admin can tap Mark complete to transition the booking to completed
  • Admin can tap Dispute to flag the claim for follow-up (sends a message to the provider thread)
  • Once actioned, the section shows who reviewed it and when
A–4

Handle a no-show and decide next steps

As an admin, I want to see the provider's reason for not showing up so I can contact the customer, reassign or cancel the job, and decide whether to log the provider no-show.

Pending UI Next sprint

Acceptance criteria

  • No-show resolution displays the provider's selected reason prominently on the booking detail
  • Quick-action buttons: Reassign · Cancel booking · Contact customer
  • Actioning any button marks the resolution as handled
  • Customer is not automatically notified — admin confirms before any outbound message is sent
A–5

See all unresolved missed jobs in one place

As an admin, I want a filtered view of bookings that are past their scheduled date and still unresolved so I can proactively follow up without relying solely on notifications.

Pending UI Next sprint

Acceptance criteria

  • Bookings list supports a Missed filter tab (past-day, committed status, no missedResolution, not completed/cancelled)
  • Each row shows: booking date, customer, assigned provider, and how many days overdue
  • Rows where the provider has submitted a resolution are visually differentiated from rows with no submission
  • Escalated jobs (clockoutFlaggedAt set) shown with a red indicator
  • Count badge on the Missed tab updates in real time as providers submit resolutions or admins action them
