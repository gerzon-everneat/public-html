How is office cleaning priced?
By square footage and frequency, quoted flat before we start — from $149/visit for small offices up to 2,000 sq ft, scaling with size and service level. No walk-up charges, no per-room surprises. We confirm the full scope and price in writing before your first visit.
Can you clean during the day, mornings, or evenings?
Yes. We schedule around your team: early mornings, evenings, overnights, or weekends, with same-day emergency response when something can't wait. Your office is never vacuumed through a meeting.
Can you provide a COI for our building?
Yes. We carry $2M in insurance and bonding and can issue a COI naming your building within 24 hours, formatted to your property manager's requirements.
How do you handle keys, alarms, and access?
Keys and fobs are logged, coded, and stored in a locked system, never labeled with your address. Alarm codes are stored encrypted, and only your assigned lead has access. Every entry and exit is time-stamped in your visit report.
Can we keep the same team every visit?
Yes. You get the same Protocol-certified lead every visit, never a rotating crew. If your lead is ever out, we brief the covering cleaner on your office's full checklist before they arrive.
What if the office isn't cleaned to standard?
Tell us within 24 hours and we come back and re-clean the areas free, no questions asked. That's the happiness guarantee.
Are your products safe around staff and clients?
Yes. Everything we use is plant-based and non-toxic: no harsh chemicals, no fumes, no lingering smell. Surfaces are touch-safe the moment we leave.
Insurance and background checks?
Every cleaner is background-checked, Protocol-certified, and W-2 employed, backed by $2M in insurance and bonding, never a random subcontractor.
