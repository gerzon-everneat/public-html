How much does a home cleaning cost?
Cleanings start at $130 per visit for weekly service. The price follows the size of your home, the level of clean (standard, deep, or move-in/out), and how often we come. You see it before you book.
What is the difference between standard, deep, and move cleaning?
Standard is regular upkeep on a recurring plan, 42 points across every room. Deep is the top-to-bottom reset most members start with: grout, fixtures and appliance fronts scrubbed, plus under-furniture and light-fixture detail. Moving preps an empty home for move-in or move-out, including tops of cabinets, closets and high shelves, with a before/after photo report.
Is your cleaning safe for my kids and pets?
Yes. Everything we use is plant-based and non-toxic, with no harsh chemicals or lingering fumes. Touch-safe the second we leave, kids and pets included.
What is included in a clean?
Every task is published in The Protocol™, numbered and room by room: living spaces, bedrooms, bathrooms, and kitchen. Pick a tier above to see exactly what's covered, and add extras like inside fridge or oven per visit.
Do I need to be home?
No, most clients aren't. Share entry instructions in chat and your Protocol-certified cleaner takes it from there. A photo report lands in your inbox before you're even back.
Do you bring your own supplies?
Yes. Our specialists bring everything, including the probiotic surface cleaner. Want to top up between visits? The same line is available to shop for home use.
Can I get the same cleaner each time?
Yes. Recurring plans come with the same trusted cleaner every visit, never a stranger. If your cleaner is ever unavailable, we tell you first and send a Protocol-certified teammate briefed on your home.
What if I am not happy with the clean?
Tell us within 24 hours and we re-clean the areas free, no questions asked. That's the happiness guarantee.
