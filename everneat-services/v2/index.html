What is probiotic cleaning?
Probiotic cleaning uses beneficial microbes to break down the organic buildup that odor-causing bacteria feed on, in grout, fabric, and soft surfaces. The microbes keep working for about 72 hours after we leave, so you get cleaner air and no chemical smell, not a masking fragrance.
How much does a cleaning cost?
Transparent hourly rates, quoted up front by unit size in chat, most homes land between $104 and $220 per visit. No surprise per-room charges, and you pay when it's done.
Is your cleaning safe for kids and pets?
Yes. Everything we use is plant-based and non-toxic, with no harsh chemicals or lingering fumes, safe around kids, pets, and sensitive surfaces.
What areas do you serve?
Everneat covers NYC (Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens), Fairfield County, CT, and New Jersey. Enter your address in chat to confirm live turnover slots near you.
What's your happiness guarantee?
If you're not thrilled with a clean, tell us within 24 hours and we re-clean the areas free, no questions asked.
What is the Everneat Protocol™?
The Protocol™ is our published, numbered checklist, every task documented room by room. You can read every point before we arrive and see a photo report of what got done after.
Do I need to provide any supplies?
No. Our specialists bring everything, including the probiotic surface cleaner. Want to top up between visits? The same line is available to shop for home use.
Are you insured, bonded, and background-checked?
Every cleaner is background-checked, Protocol-certified, and W-2 employed, backed by $2M in insurance and bonding, never a random subcontractor.
