How fast is a turnover?
We build a buffer into every turnover window and track your check-out and check-in times. If a guest runs late, we re-sequence the route and keep you posted in real time, and we flag immediately if the gap puts your next check-in at risk, so you can decide together.
Do you send proof before check-in?
Yes. A photo report and inventory count land in your inbox before your guest checks in, so you see the listing is guest-ready without setting foot in it. Damage or low stock triggers an alert the moment we find it.
Can you handle back-to-back and same-day bookings?
Yes. Host plans include priority same-day windows, and iCal / PMS sync schedules us automatically the moment a check-out lands. Peak weekends are reserved for plan members first, so you never miss a booking.
Do you launder and stage linens?
Yes. Beds are stripped and remade with fresh linens, towel sets staged, and laundry run on-site or rotated off-site depending on your setup. Linens come back lint-rolled and hotel-crisp.
What if a guest leaves damage or runs low on supplies?
Every turnover ends with an inspection: we photograph and report any damage and count essentials and consumables. You get an alert before the next guest arrives, in time to claim, restock, or reschedule.
Is your cleaning safe for guests right after check-in?
Yes. Everything we use is probiotic and plant-based, with no harsh chemicals or lingering fumes. Guests walk into clean air, not a chemical smell, and the shield keeps working for about 72 hours.
How is turnover pricing set?
Transparent hourly rates by unit size, from $65/hr for solo hosts down to $52/hr for enterprise portfolios. The matrix above shows every size; the quote is the price, no surprise per-room charges.
Are your cleaners insured and background-checked?
Every cleaner is background-checked, Protocol-certified, and W-2 employed, backed by $2M in insurance and bonding, never a random subcontractor between guests.
