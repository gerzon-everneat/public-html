What to expect after we leave

The microbes keep working long after we leave. For about 72 hours they compete with the bacteria that cause smells, reaching into grout, fabric, and the soft surfaces a wipe-down never touches. The result is cleaner indoor air, not a masking fragrance.

Conventional products strip a surface and evaporate. Probiotic formulas leave beneficial microbes behind, and those microbes digest the organic residue that odor-causing bacteria feed on. Nothing to feed on means nothing to smell.
