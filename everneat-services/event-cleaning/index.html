How fast can you mobilize?
Same-day for events under 72 hours out. For larger productions we recommend a 5-day lead so we can scope the venue, walk the space, and brief the crew. Either way, the quote and scope are agreed in writing before the date.
Do you handle spills, broken glass, or messes mid-event?
Yes, that's what live service is for. Your on-site team stays through the event: spills wiped, glass swept and bagged, bins swapped, and restrooms restocked before guests notice anything happened.
Can you do load-out and full venue restoration?
Yes. The post-event reset covers the full strike: deep floor care, waste removal, and vendor handoff. We hand the venue back in walkthrough condition, photographed, so your deposit isn't a question mark.
Can you provide a COI for our venue?
Yes. We carry $2M in insurance and bonding and can issue a COI naming your venue within 24 hours, formatted to your venue manager's requirements.
How many specialists will be on-site?
From 2 for a single-day pop-up to 20 for a full production. We staff to your guest count, venue size, and timeline, and the headcount is confirmed in your quote before the date.
What if something isn't right on the day?
Flag your on-site lead and it's handled in the moment. Anything after the event, tell us within 24 hours and we come back and make it right, free. That's the happiness guarantee.
Are your products safe around guests and food service?
Yes. Everything we use is plant-based and non-toxic: no harsh chemicals, no fumes, no lingering smell. Surfaces are touch-safe the moment they're treated, including food-service areas.
Insurance and background checks?
Every cleaner is background-checked, Protocol-certified, and W-2 employed, backed by $2M in insurance and bonding, never a random subcontractor.
